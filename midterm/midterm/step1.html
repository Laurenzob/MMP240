Critical Role cast

Critical Role is a show where 8 of some of the most popular voice actors from video games or animated shows alike, meet up and play Dungeons and Dragons. The combination of their amazing acting skills with the chemistry they all have together make the show very enjoyable to watch, especially with the brilliant mind of their DM (Storyteller) Matthew Mercer.
